About Our School

Shashank Public Senior Secondary School, established in Mundwara, Rajasthan, is dedicated to providing a nurturing and stimulating environment for students from diverse backgrounds. Our mission is to foster intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and a strong sense of community.

The school was constructed on **October 9, 2011**, by the **Jangid community of Brahmans**, and has since been a beacon of education in the region. We believe in a holistic approach to education, combining rigorous academic programs with a wide range of extracurricular activities. Our experienced and passionate faculty are committed to guiding each student towards their full potential, preparing them not just for examinations, but for life.

The school boasts modern facilities, including well-equipped classrooms, science and computer labs, a library, and sports amenities, all designed to support a comprehensive learning experience.

How to Get Admission

Admission to Shashank Public Senior Secondary School is open to students who meet our eligibility criteria. We encourage prospective students and their parents to visit our campus to learn more about our programs and facilities.

Admission Process:

  1. Enquiry: Contact our admission office via phone or email, or fill out the online enquiry form on our website (if available).
  2. Application Form: Obtain the admission application form from the school office or download it from our website.
  3. Submission: Submit the completed application form along with all required documents (birth certificate, previous school records, photographs, etc.) by the specified deadline.
  4. Entrance Test/Interview: Depending on the grade level, students may be required to appear for an entrance test or an interview.
  5. Admission Confirmation: Successful candidates will be notified, and parents will be required to complete the admission formalities, including fee payment.

For detailed information on eligibility, required documents, and important dates, please contact our admission counselor at the number provided below.

Activities at Shashank Public School

At Shashank Public Senior Secondary School, we believe in a vibrant and engaging learning environment that extends beyond the classroom. We offer a variety of activities to foster creativity, teamwork, and personal growth.

  • Mock Drills: Regular mock drills are conducted to ensure student safety and preparedness for various emergency situations.
  • Saturday Activities: The 1st and 3rd Saturdays of every month are dedicated to fun and educational activities, including clubs, workshops, and inter-house competitions.
  • Yearly Olympiad Trips: High-achieving students in our annual Olympiads get the exciting opportunity to participate in educational trips to prestigious locations like N.A.S.A. and London.
  • Annual Picnics: We organize yearly picnics to various scenic spots around Delhi, Haryana, and Rajasthan, providing a refreshing break and fostering camaraderie among students.

These activities are designed to provide a well-rounded educational experience, helping students discover new interests, develop essential life skills, and create lasting memories.

Sports at Shashank Public School

Physical fitness and sportsmanship are integral to our curriculum. Shashank Public Senior Secondary School offers a wide array of sports facilities and training to encourage students to excel in various athletic pursuits.

Our Sports Offerings Include:

  • Football
  • Badminton
  • Cricket
  • Handball
  • Netball
  • Basketball
  • Judo
  • Boxing
  • And many more!

Students have the opportunity to participate in yearly zonal competitions, showcasing their talents and competing against other schools. Our dedicated coaches provide expert guidance, giving students the chance to progress to state and even national-level championships. We believe sports teach valuable lessons in discipline, teamwork, and perseverance.
